Why be a fellow

“Life doesn't get better by chance, it gets better by change”. And the change starts with YOU! India has got 600 million young people and an equivalent force is needed to bring a massive change for the betterment of the nation. There is a huge difference between thinking about changing the world and trying to change it instead. iCFDR fellowship allows you to change the world by participating in the most important social work: Transforming the Future of India. This can be attained by educating the downtrodden and children belonging to marginalized communities. 670 million of India’s population have no big dream than to attain two meals a day. In this wretched state, we can't imagine the kids of these communities to dream about education. You as a Fellow will help them all, by igniting their dreams with the spark of knowledge.

About the Fellowship

The iCFDR fellowship program will be for twelve months. The program makes sure to transform these young individuals into an excellent leader with futuristic skills.A fellow is expected to enthusiastically and constructively participate in the following events:

  • Working with iCFDR as a teacher and to follow the standardised curriculum prescribed.
  • Should be able to deal empathetically with all the kids, compassionately accepting who they are and supporting them through thick and thin.
  • Should be able to carefully analyse the psychology of the children.
  • Should not be restricting their world to books, but boosting up their creative side which is concealed under the sheets of misery, poverty and dejection.
  • Should focus on the holistic development of the child.
  • Should teach the important life skills needed to be a better human.

The fellow will be informed before the classes to be taken with detailed information of the whereabouts/location.

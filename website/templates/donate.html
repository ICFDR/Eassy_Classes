Why should you donate?

  • To help the kids from low-income families have hope.
  • To us motivate childrens in slums to work hard and have positive outlook.
  • To help make them feel a part of society and improve their quality of life.
  • To assist us focusing on girl children in the slums.
  • To lighten up someone’s life.
  • To be the reason a child smiles in gratitude and a homeless person knows warmth for a change.
  • To prevent young children from dropping out of school and missing out on a lifetime of opportunities.


Where does the sponsorship money go?

When you donate a little out of your resources, it is utilised for our operation of Easy Classes and communities we are working with. iCFDR is out on a mission to lend a helping hand to the poor and destitute. It has been actively involved in rendering services to underprivileged children, with our Easy Classes program. We need your cooperation and support in carrying out the good community work. Your sponsorship and donation will be an ‘anchor to our ship of noble work.’ It will help us reach out to the people in need and expand our areas of social work with them.




Impact of donation

  • Your donation will transform the lives of many and give them hope and motivation to turn around their life.
  • You will empower and energise people for future prospects.
  • You will contribute to shaping the leaders of tomorrow.
  • You will be the source of happiness in their lives
  • You will help them live a decent life.
  • You will help us sustain our social work with childrens in slums.


What do you get in return?

  • Joy of being part of supporters of iCFDR to help the kids in the slums.
  • Exemption as per Income Tax Act 1961.
  • Stay connected with us to get regular updates of our work.

BONUS: The heartfelt blessings and good wishes of all the people whose lives you touch with your kindness and give them a ray of hope in their bleak world.
